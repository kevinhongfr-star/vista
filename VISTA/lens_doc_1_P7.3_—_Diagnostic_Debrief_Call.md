## HOW TO TALK ABOUT OUR DIAGNOSTICS

Our diagnostic methodology is based on decades of organizational psychology research. While the specific tools are new applications of established frameworks, the underlying principles are well-validated:

- SPARK draws on AI readiness and technology adoption research

- BRIDGE is grounded in cross-cultural management research (Hofstede, Meyer, Earley & Mosakowski)

- MOSAIC is based on team composition and dynamics research

- FORGE draws on corporate governance and decision-making research

We're currently validating these diagnostics with pilot clients. When discussing them:

- Say: 'Our methodology is based on established research in [area]'

- Say: 'We're currently validating the framework with pilot clients'

- Don't say: 'This has been tested with 500+ companies'

- Don't say: 'Companies see X% improvement' without citing specific research
