## SECTION S9 — Appendix

### A. Diagnostic Methodology Summary

SPARK — AI Leadership Readiness

SPARK measures five signals of AI leadership readiness: AI Literacy, Governance Judgment, Strategic Capability, Behavioral Adaptability, and Team & Culture. Each signal is weighted by predictive impact. The assessment takes 15–25 minutes and generates a composite score with dimension-level breakdown and benchmark comparison.

BRIDGE — Cross-Border Governance

BRIDGE measures six dimensions of cross-border governance readiness: Board Readiness, Intelligence, Decision-rights, Governance, Execution, and Partnership Dynamics. Designed for leaders operating across multiple markets or cultural contexts.

MOSAIC — Team Cohesion

MOSAIC measures five dimensions of team operating cohesion: Management, Operating-norms, Style-calibration, Alignment, and Intercultural-cohesion. Applicable to any leadership team regardless of cultural composition.

FORGE — Revenue Architecture

FORGE measures six dimensions of revenue organization health: Forecasting, Operations, Revenue-architecture, Growth-execution, Enablement, and Growth-enablement. Applicable to any organization with revenue accountability.

### B. Confidentiality Terms

All diagnostic data, findings, and engagement materials are treated as strictly confidential. LYC Partners will not share client-specific data with third parties without explicit written consent. Aggregate benchmarking data may be used anonymously to improve diagnostic calibration.

### C. Cancellation Policy

- Diagnostics: Non-refundable once assessment links have been issued.

- Programs: 50% refund if cancelled more than 30 days before program start; no refund within 30 days.

- Advisory retainers: 30-day written notice required to terminate. Final month's retainer is non-refundable.

- Workshops: 50% refund if cancelled more than 14 days before the workshop date; no refund within 14 days.
