## Diagnostic-Specific Debrief Sequences

### SPARK — AI Readiness

1. Composite score → overall AI readiness signal

1. AI Literacy (×0.20) → knowledge baseline

1. Governance Judgment (×0.25) → risk/ethics orientation

1. Strategic Capability (×0.25) → vision and application

1. Behavioral Adaptability (×0.15) → change readiness

1. Team & Culture (×0.15) → environment for AI adoption

Key probe: "Which of these five signals surprises you most?"

### BRIDGE — Cross-Border Governance

1. Composite score → cross-border governance readiness

1. Board Readiness (×0.20) → governance structure

1. Intelligence (×0.15) → market/context awareness

1. Decision-rights (×0.15) → clarity of authority

1. Governance (×0.20) → framework strength

1. Execution (×0.15) → delivery track record

Key probe: "Where does your cross-border decision chain most commonly break down?"

### MOSAIC — Team Cohesion

1. Composite score → team operating cohesion

1. Management (×0.25) → leadership style

1. Operating-norms (×0.20) → working agreements

1. Style-calibration (×0.20) → flexibility and adaptation

1. Alignment (×0.20) → shared direction

1. Intercultural-cohesion (×0.15) → cross-cultural effectiveness

Key probe: "If you could change one operating norm on this team tomorrow, what would it be?"

### FORGE — Revenue Architecture

1. Composite score → revenue architecture health

1. Forecasting (×0.20) → prediction accuracy

1. Operations (×0.20) → process discipline

1. Revenue-architecture (×0.25) → structural design

1. Growth-execution (×0.12) → delivery track record

1. Enablement (×0.15) → team capability support

1. Growth-enablement (×0.08) → scaling readiness

Key probe: "Is the gap in your revenue org a people problem, a process problem, or a structure problem?"

## Decision Tree: When...

Client engaged but not ready to commit

→ Offer the thinking session: "30 minutes next week, no commitment."

→ Send Decision Brief same day.

Client wants to expand scope

→ Reference Bundle options. "If we structured this as Bundle #[X], you'd get [components] at a saving of [X%]."

Client raises budget concern during debrief

→ Open ROI Calculator. Walk through Cost of Inaction together first.

→ "Before we talk about investment, let's agree on what the problem is costing you."

Sponsor present but final decision-maker is not

→ Prepare Decision Brief during the call: "I'll have this in your inbox by end of day."

→ "What does [decision-maker] care most about — governance risk, talent pipeline, or revenue predictability?"

Client challenges the diagnostic data

→ Welcome it: "That's exactly the right reaction. Tell me what you're seeing that's different."

→ Use their challenge to deepen discovery. Data disagreement is still engagement.
